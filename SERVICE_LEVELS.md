# Service Levels (SLA-light)

## Response targets
- New inbound request acknowledgment: within 2 business days.
- Active project async reply target: within 2 business days.

## Delivery model
- Best-effort professional service, no 24/7 on-call in standard packages.
- Time-critical priority slots may be available as add-on.

## Support window
- Typical post-delivery async support: 3–6 business days.


## Complexity note
Support and turnaround depend on fix complexity; target range is typically 3–6 business days, often faster when feasible.
